About Healthifier

Healthifier is the one stop solution for all your calorie requirements. Sign in and we will keep your calorie counts.

A food tracker and a workout tracker keeps track and calculates the number of calories you gain and lose everyday. You can also generate a detailed report summary that helps you visualize your current lifestyle (food and workout habits) and plan them accordingly.

Lastly, we have a notify section where you can notify if we miss on a food/workout item, or how do you like our app, or request any new/existing feature changes.
